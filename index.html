Welcome to suduko solver follow the instructions below to begin.

  1. Input the current position to solve for by click a number and the click the square the the number is located in.
  2. Click Solve current position to show one move ahead.
  3. Click Solve Puzzle to complete the puzzle.
  4. Now transfer over the solved puzzle to your app and see how well it worked.

If you input a puzzle with multiple possible answers the output will be an Invaid Puzzle
